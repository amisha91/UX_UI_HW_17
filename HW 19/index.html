Overview

The student loan debt accounts for over a trillion dollars in America. Students are burdened to pay back loans, without actually having formal education in the pros and cons of borrowing a large sum of money. This app is designed to help young people learn about student loans and manage their current debt.

Reserach

The inspiration behind this app is the overwhelming responses from our survey stating, “I feel like I’m not financially literate so my debt is very burdening.” Many students feel as though they were not prepared for taking out large sums of money. In order to prepare for that, we have created an app that allows students learn the nuances of student loan debt and how to mange that debt.
